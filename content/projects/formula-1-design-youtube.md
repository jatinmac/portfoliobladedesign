I built this channel from scratch around Formula 1 design and visual storytelling.

The focus was creating consistent niche content around a subject I care about deeply.

It grew past 1 million views, showing my ability to package ideas clearly and build audience interest over time.

## Problem

The challenge was to build attention around a niche subject from zero. Formula 1 design is visually rich, but audience growth required more than interest in the topic; the content had to package ideas clearly, create a recognizable point of view, and make design stories accessible to viewers.

## My Role & Responsibility

I owned the channel as a self-initiated side project. That included choosing the niche, shaping content strategy, developing the visual storytelling approach, publishing consistently, and learning what audiences responded to over time.

## Design Process

The process worked like a product-growth loop: identify a specific audience interest, turn that interest into repeatable content formats, publish, observe response, and refine the next video. The design work was not only visual; it was also about framing, sequencing, titles, packaging, and consistency.

## Key Design Decisions

- Stay focused on Formula 1 design and visual storytelling instead of broad automotive content, because a clear niche is easier for an audience to understand.
- Package ideas visually and narratively so design details could become accessible content.
- Treat consistency as part of the product experience: recurring themes and recognizable framing helped build audience trust.
- Use audience response as feedback for what to make clearer in the next piece of content.

## Outcome and Impact

The channel grew from zero to more than 1 million views. This makes it useful evidence for audience understanding, idea packaging, content strategy, and the ability to build momentum around a focused creative product.

## What I Learned

Audience growth rewards clarity and repetition. The project taught me how positioning, visual explanation, and consistent publishing can turn a niche interest into a scalable content system.
